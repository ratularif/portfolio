Who I Am

About Me

A passionate professional who believes in continuous growth, collaboration, and creating real impact.

I'm Md. Arif Billah Chowdhury, born in 1992 and based in Joypurhat, Bangladesh. My career spans over 6 years across diverse industries — from financial fashion to retail footwear to premium banking.

My journey began in accounts and finance at The Delta Quality Fashions, where I developed a solid foundation in financial operations. This hands-on experience with numbers gave me a unique edge when I transitioned into sales and marketing — I understood profitability, margins, and business viability in ways most sales professionals don't.

At HDF Footwear Nabik Ltd, I transformed that financial knowledge into sales performance — closing deals, presenting to clients, collecting orders, and nurturing long-term relationships over 3+ years. Finally, at Padma Bank PLC, I brought both skill sets together as an Associate Relationship Officer — delivering premium banking experiences while expanding and retaining the bank's client base.

My core philosophy? "Learning never stops — from people, cultures, and every new experience." I believe the best professionals are those who stay curious, stay hungry, and always put the client first.
